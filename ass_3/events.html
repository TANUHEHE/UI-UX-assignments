3 Days of Fun, Culture & Celebration!

Day 1: The Grand Kickoff

  • Inaugural Ceremony - Lamp lighting, opening address, and theme reveal
  • Battle of Bands - Rock, pop and indie groups setting the stage on fire
  • Street Play (Nukkad Natak) - Powerful performances with social messages
  • Art & Photography Exhibitions - Campus turns into a gallery
  • Pro Night 1 - DJ & EDM Night for a high-energy start

Day 2: The Cultural Explosion

  • Dance Showdown - Solo, duet and crew battles across styles
  • Singing Competition - From soulful classics to pop hits

Day 3: The Grand Finale

  • Morning Yoga & Wellness Workshop - A refreshing start to the finale
  • Tech Talks & Startup Pitches - Innovative ideas and entrepreneurial spirit
  • Open Mic & Slam Poetry - Words that inspire and entertain
  • Drama & Fashion Show - Lights, costumes and glamour
  • Closing Ceremony & Prize Distribution - Celebrating the champions
  • Pro Night 3 - Live band performance with fireworks and farewell beats
